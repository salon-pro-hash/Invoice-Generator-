Our Story

About Invoice Generator

Invoice Generator was built to solve a simple problem: freelancers, agencies, and small businesses need a fast, elegant way to send professional invoices without paying for bloated accounting software or wrestling with clunky templates. We started as a weekend project focused on one idea — billing should feel effortless, not administrative.

Every part of the tool runs directly in your browser. Your company details, client records, and saved invoices are stored locally on your own device, which means your financial data is never uploaded to a server we control. That gives you speed, privacy, and full ownership of your billing history in one lightweight page.

We designed the interface around three principles borrowed from the products we admire most: the clarity of Apple, the precision of Stripe's dashboards, and the friendliness of Notion. The result is a premium black, white, and gold aesthetic that looks at home whether you're invoicing a boutique client or a Fortune 500 vendor.

What makes it different

  • No sign-up required. Open the page and start invoicing immediately — there's no account wall between you and your first invoice.
  • Works offline. Once loaded, the editor, calculator, and PDF export all function without an internet connection.
  • Unlimited invoices. Save, search, duplicate, and edit as many invoices as you need, all stored locally in your browser.
  • Global currency support. Bill clients in USD, EUR, GBP, PKR, AED, SAR, INR, CAD, AUD, or JPY with correct symbol formatting.
  • Print-perfect PDFs. Every export is laid out to precise A4 dimensions, ready for email or physical mailing.

Frequently asked questions

Is Invoice Generator really free to use?

Yes. All core features — unlimited invoices, PDF export, printing, dark mode, and local invoice history — are completely free with no hidden charges.

Where is my invoice data stored?

All invoice data is saved in your browser's local storage on your own device. Nothing is transmitted to an external server, so clearing your browser data will remove your saved invoices.

Can I use this on my phone or tablet?

Absolutely. The entire interface is fully responsive and has been tuned for mobile, tablet, and desktop screens alike.

Does the PDF download include the buttons and toolbar?

No. The PDF export captures only the clean invoice document itself, formatted precisely for A4 printing, with no interface elements included.

Can I add my own logo and signature?

Yes. You can drag and drop or upload a logo and a signature image, both of which appear on the live preview and the final PDF.
